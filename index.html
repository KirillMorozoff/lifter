Lifter

The app "Lifter" calculates an individual training program.

All metrics given on the basis of the most advanced methodological developments in the field of powerlifting and physiology.

The application used programs such eminent athletes and methodists as Ted Arcidi and Yuri Verkhoshansky.

BenchPress

Exercise should be done up to 2 times a week. Before the exercise, perform a warm-up and the first approach should be with a blank fretboard to avoid injuries.

Enter the max weight you can push at 1 time.

Enter the number of days per week that you can train this exercise.
